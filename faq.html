• FAQs
What is PhotoPuddle?

Photopuddle is a medium through which we provide photo editing services of professional/amateur photo editors to our customers. This service enhances the quality of photographs and adds smiles to memories.

When do I have to pay?
No payments are required for uploading photos and getting them edited. Once edited, you may view the edited photos and only then do you have to pay to download the photos.

Can I get a refund?
Since you only pay for the photos you choose, we do not entertain refund requests.

Is there any SignUp or registration required?
There is no signup or registration required for the customers. Exchange of photos or any sort of communication will be done just through your email id.

Are my uploaded photos safe?
Our legal privacy policy states that we are not allowed to share or make copies of your photos under any circumstances. So remain assured, your photos are in safe hands. Also, none of the photos that you upload will be used for our publicity without your permission.



How long will it take to get my photos edited?
We try to keep all our customers happy and satisfied. Even though we do not provide or guarentee any fixed time to get back your edited photos, but we assure you, you will be notified as soon as the work is done. And it would be sooner than you think!.

Can I choose the editor I want?
All our editor profiles will be available to you along with examples of their previous works. You can choose whichever editor you like and he/she will be assigned to your project accordingly.

Can I have my photos edited as per my requirements?
We have a reasonably flexible system where the customers can contact their respective editors through email and give in their requirements may that be special effects or anything of such kind. There is also a text box below the upload option wherein customers can provide specifications.

Will I have to pay for all the photographs that I upload?
All your photos will be edited the best way possible. You can pay and download only those photos which you like or require while the rest will be discarded. You need not pay for all the photos in your project.

Is there any limit on the no of photos I upload?
There is no limit on the number of photos you upload. All of your photos will be edited and you can select, pay and downoad only the ones you like.

How will I be notified that my photos have been edited?
Photos will be edited in batches of few. As soon as a batch has been edited you will be notified via email and you can download them.
